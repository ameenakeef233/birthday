Today is a very special day for our pretty princess, Siti Nur Farhanah. 🤍 A day to celebrate someone who deserves nothing but the most beautiful things life could ever offer. May all your wishes slowly come true, one by one, in the most beautiful and unexpected ways possible. Moga awak menjadi insan yang lebih bahagia, lebih kuat, dan sentiasa diberikan ketenangan dalam setiap perkara yang awak jalani selepas ini. I will always be by your side, walaupun disaat semesta tidak memihak sekalipun. Because honestly, seeing you happy has always been one of my favourite things. And honestly, no matter how much time passes, there will always be a special place for you inside my heart. Happy Birthday once again, my pretty princess. 🤍 May this new age bring you closer to everything beautiful your heart has ever wished for. May your days always be filled with love, warmth, comfort, and people who truly appreciate your existence. May Allah protected you from sadness that is too heavy for your heart to carry. And no matter what happens in life, I will always wish the very best for you. Always. 💖
